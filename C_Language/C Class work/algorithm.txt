Algorithms:

A process which follows step by step is called an Algorithm.

E.g Making a Tea


Step 1) Start   --->> Ovel


Step 2) Input   --->> Parallelogram


Step 3) Process --->> Rectangle


Step 4) Decision -if yes goto next step, if no goto step1. --->> Diomond


Step 5) Output


Step 6) Stop
